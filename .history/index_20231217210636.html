What We Do

Launched in 2015, Awake Youth Initiative has two programs: Awake Lead Young program, the 8 months leadership, entrepreneurship and financial literacy training is rooted in Dream It! Do It! Grow It! leadership training toolkit and has reached 2,733+ young people in Nairobi’s underserved communities. The program has facilitated workplace diversity, self-employment and linkage to over 500 youth with amazing skills set untapped for recruitment. 11 of the alumni are running social enterprises transforming communities at grassroot level.

Our Story

Meshack Otieno founded Awake Youth Initiative in 2015 with a vision of Transforming youth livelihood and leadership in underserved communities. Growing up in Mathare, the second largest slum in Kenya with an approximate population of 500,000 people, Meshack experienced extreme poverty, violence, and lack of opportunity. However, he also witnessed palpable hope that persist in slum and recognized that youths had sought something different for themselves. Through his mentor Vincent Odhiambo (Regional Director, Ashoka Africa), Meshack had learnt that greater opportunity could be created from within. In 2014, He graduated from Lepta community, an organization that trains youths on Leadership and entrepreneur skills, joined Ashoka East Africa Youth Venture where he grew his network and in 2015 he started Awake Youth Initiative in Mathare. Through Ashoka, Meshack met Tamara Quinlan, a passionate changemaker who was ready to channel her expertise and strength to help scale Awake. Together they joined hands to steer the vision ahead.
