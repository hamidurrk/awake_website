A-Team

MESHACK OTIENO - CO-FOUNDER & C.E.O

Introducing Meshack, the Co-founder and C.E.O of Awake Youth Initiative, a driving force behind the organization's remarkable growth and impact.

Under Meshack's visionary leadership, Awake Youth Initiative has achieved tremendous milestones. From raising $50,000 to empowering 2,733 young changemakers at the grassroots level, Meshack has been at the forefront of driving positive change.

As an SDG 1 Global Youth Ambassador with SDGuild, Meshack is actively working to develop campus points for measuring the attainment of SDG 1, ensuring progress towards eradicating poverty and promoting sustainable development.

Meshack's dedication to equity in underserved communities is evident through their role as a champion for the We Are Family Foundation's Youth To The Front Fund. By developing changemakers, Meshack is making a significant impact on creating a more equitable world.

As a young leader of The Queen's Commonwealth Trust, Meshack continues to inspire and drive change across East Africa. Their storytelling skills have proven instrumental in amplifying stories of change and progress in the region.

Recognized for their outstanding efforts, Meshack was awarded the 2019 National Diversity and Inclusion Award for promoting Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion at Awake Youth Initiative.

Notably, Meshack's dedication to innovation and community impact was recognized when they won the International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent's 2022 Limitless Challenge. This global competition brought together over 1,000 innovators, designing solutions to various challenges in their communities.

Beyond their role at Awake, Meshack has been actively involved as a Kenya Red Cross Volunteer, showcasing their commitment to making a difference in society. Their expertise in network building and experience in civic and social organizations is testament to their dedication to fostering positive change.

A passionate storyteller, Meshack shares stories of change across East Africa, inspiring and empowering the next generation of changemakers.

With a relentless drive for progress and a passion for empowering the youth, Meshack continues to lead Awake Youth Initiative towards a brighter and more equitable future.

BRYAN CHESOLI – PROGRAM DESIGN, MONITORING & EVALUATION

Bryan Chesoli is a seasoned project manager with extensive experience leading transformative initiatives in the education and health sector. As a child and youth program manager at Ashoka East Africa, Bryan championed efforts to revolutionize teaching and learning in Kenya, Uganda and Tanzania through Transforming Employability for Social Change in East

Africa. In this capacity, Bryan designed a training toolkit that improved the teaching and learning experience in over four universities. Additionally, Bryan has played a vital role in the Making More Health Accelerator (MMHA) program which is focused on searching, selecting and supporting social entrepreneurs who tackle challenges in human and animal health. The accelerator offers support over a period of 12 months for pilot funding of up to 50,000 Euros and a further 12 months for scale-up funding of up to 200,000 Euros. Since joining the accelerator, Bryan has led initiatives to improve the MMH accelerator by streamlining knowledge-sharing, improving the search, selection and vetting of applicants, conducting site visits for due diligence, leading the contracting of selected ventures and helping curate meaningful engagement between beneficiaries of the accelerator and industry experts to support selected ventures to address pain-points that impeded their growth.

Bryan is joining the Board to assist the team to streamline its programs and ensure best practices in monitoring and evaluation.
