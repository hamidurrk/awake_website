65% of young people in undeserved communities face a significant gap in leadership skills, entrepreneurship acumen, and financial literacy, limiting their ability to navigate and contribute meaningfully to an increasingly complex and competitive global landscape. This gap not only hinders personal development but also poses a barrier to societal progress as these skills are crucial for innovation, economic growth, and community leadership.

Our Solution

The Awake Lead Young Program was designed to bridge this gap by providing comprehensive training in leadership, entrepreneurship, and financial literacy. Through a 5-month intensive program, participants are equipped with the skills, knowledge, and confidence needed to become effective changemakers and entrepreneurs. The program aims to empower young individuals with the tools to navigate challenges, make informed decisions, and contribute positively to their communities.

Courses

Social Entrepreneurship

This transformative program is designed for aspiring changemakers who envision a world where business meets social impact. Social entrepreneurship combines the innovation and dynamism of entrepreneurship with a profound commitment to addressing societal challenges.

Desired Outcomes

01
A cohort of empowered and capable changemakers who are not only adept at leading others but also possess the entrepreneurial mindset needed to drive innovation and the financial literacy to make informed economic decisions.
02
Participants should emerge with a sense of purpose, resilience, and the ability to create positive change in both their personal and professional lives.
03
Changemakers launch their start-up ventures in their communities.

Short term goals (1-2 years)

1. Skill Acquisition: Ensure participants acquire fundamental skills in leadership, entrepreneurship, and financial literacy through a combination of workshops, mentorship, and practical exercises.
2. Network Building: Facilitate networking opportunities for participants to connect with mentors, peers, and industry professionals, fostering a supportive community for ongoing learning and collaboration.
3. Project Implementation: Encourage participants to apply their acquired skills by initiating and implementing social enterprises, allowing them to practice leadership and entrepreneurial concepts in real-world scenarios.

Long term goals (3-5 years)

1. Leadership in Diverse Fields: Witness participants assuming leadership roles in diverse sectors, showcasing the applicability of their skills in various professional settings.
2. Entrepreneurial Ventures: Support and track the establishment of businesses and entrepreneurial ventures by program alumni, contributing to economic growth and job creation.
3. Community Impact: Measure the program's impact on communities served by participants, evaluating positive changes and sustainable initiatives resulting from their leadership and entrepreneurial activities.
4. Program Expansion: Scale the Awake Lead Young Program to reach a broader audience, either by increasing the number of participants or by expanding into new regions, thereby multiplying its positive influence.
